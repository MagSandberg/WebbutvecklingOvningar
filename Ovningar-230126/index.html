En simpel butik

A C# console program for shopping that allows users to purchase items through a command-line interface.

The program displays a list of available products, including their names, prices, and descriptions. Users can select items to add to their cart, view the contents of their cart, and check out to complete their purchase. The program utilizes C# classes and objects to represent products and the shopping cart, and implements basic input validation to ensure that the user enters valid commands and data. It also allows the user to add or remove items, update the quantities of items in the cart, and also has a feature to checkout and calculate the total amount of the cart. Overall, the program provides a simple and user-friendly way to shop for products through a command-line interface.
